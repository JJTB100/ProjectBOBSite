Project B.O.B

Project B.O.B is a maze game where you are trapped inside a university and have to get out before the time runs out. There are hidden events and events that can increase or decrease your score as you go along. The aim is to escape in the time limit and get the highest score possible, the time you complete it in will impact your score so escape fast! Bob is chasing you so make sure not to get caught.
